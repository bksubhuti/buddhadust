Saɱyutta Nikāya
5. Mahā-Vagga
56. Sacca Saɱyutta
5. Papāta Vagga

Sutta 46

Andhakāra Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[454]

[1][pts][bodh][than] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Atthi bhikkhave, lokantarikā aghā asaɱvutā andhakārakā andhakāratimisā.|| ||

Yatthā-pimesaɱ candima-suriyānaɱ evaɱ-mahiddhikānaɱ evaɱ-mah-ā-nubhāvānaɱ ābhā nānubhontī" ti.|| ||

Evaɱ vutte aññataro bhikkhu Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Mahā vata so bhante, andhakāro.|| ||

Sumahā vata so bhante, andhakāro.|| ||

Atthi nukho bhante, etamhā andhakārā añño andhakāro mahantataro ca bhayānakataro cā" ti?|| ||

"Atthi kho bhikkhu, etamhā andhakārā añño andhakāro mahantataro ca bhayānakataro cā" ti.|| ||

"Katamo pana bhante, etamhā andhakārā añño andhakāro mahantaro ca bhayānakataro cā" ti?|| ||

"Ye hi keci bhikkhu samaṇā vā brāhmaṇā vā||
'idaɱ dukkhan' ti||
yathā-bhūtaɱ na pajānanti.|| ||

'Ayaɱ dukkha-samudayo' ti||
yathā-bhūtaɱ na pajānanti.|| ||

'Ayaɱ dukkha-nirodho' ti||
yathā-bhūtaɱ na pajānanti.|| ||

'Ayaɱ dukkha-nirodha-gāminī [455] paṭipadā' ti||
yathā-bhūtaɱ na pajānanti.|| ||

Te jāti-saŋvaṭṭa-nikesu sankhāresu abhiramanti.|| ||

Jarā-saŋvaṭṭa-nikesu sankhāresu abhiramanti.|| ||

Maraṇa-saŋvaṭṭa-nikesu sankhāresu abhiramanti.|| ||

Soka-parideva-dukkha-domanass'upāyāsa-saŋvaṭṭa-nikesu sankhāresu abhiramanti.|| ||

Te jāti-saŋvaṭṭa-nikesu sankhāresu abhiratā||
jarā-saŋvaṭṭa-nikesu sankhāresu abhiratā||
maraṇa-saŋvaṭṭa-nikesu sankhāresu abhiratā||
soka-parideva-dukkha-domanass'upayā-sasaŋvaṭṭa-nikesu sankhāresu abhiratā||
jāti-saŋvaṭṭa-nike pi sankhāre abhisankhāronti.|| ||

Jarā-saŋvaṭṭa-nike pi sankhāre abhisankhāroti.|| ||

Maraṇa-saŋvaṭṭa-nike pi sankhāre abhisankhāronti.|| ||

Soka-parideva-dukkha-domanass'upāyāsa-saŋvaṭṭa-nike pi sankhāre abhisankhāronti.|| ||

Te jāti-saŋvaṭṭa-nike pi sankhāre abhisankhāritvā jarā-saŋvaṭṭa-nike pi sankhāre abhisankhāritvā||
maraṇa-saŋvaṭṭa-nike pi sankhāre abhisankhāritvā||
soka-parideva-dukkha-domanass'upāyāsa-saŋvaṭṭa-nike pi sankhāre abhisankhāritvā||
jāt'andhakāram pi papatanti||
jar'andhakāram pi papatanti||
māraṇ'andhakāram pi papatanti||
soka-parideva-dukkha-domanass'upāyās'andhakāram pi papatanti.|| ||

'Te na parimuccanti||
jātiyā jarāya maraṇena sokehi paridevehi dukkhehi domanassehi upāyāsehi,||
na parimuccanti dukkhasmā' ti vadāmi.|| ||

 

§

 

Ye ca kho keci bhikkhu samaṇā vā brāhmaṇā vā||
'idaɱ dukkhan' ti||
yathā-bhūtaɱ pajānanti||
'ayaɱ dukkha-samudayo' ti||
yathā-bhūtaɱ pajānanti||
'ayaɱ dukkha-nirodho' ti||
yathā-bhūtaɱ pajānanti||
'ayaɱ dukkha-nirodha-gāminī paṭipadā' ti||
yathā-bhūtaɱ pajānanti.|| ||

Te jāti-saŋvaṭṭa-nikesu sankhāresu nābhiramanti.|| ||

Jarā-saŋvaṭṭa-nikesu sankhāresu nābhiramanti.|| ||

Maraṇa-saŋvaṭṭa-nikesu sankhāresu nābhiramanti.|| ||

Soka-parideva-dukkha-domanass'upāyāsa-saŋvaṭṭa-nikesu sankhāresu nābhiramanti.|| ||

Te jāti-saŋvaṭṭa-nikesu sankhāresu anabhiratā||
jarā-saŋvaṭṭa-nikesu sankhāresu anabhiratā||
maraṇa-saŋvaṭṭa-nikesu sankhāresu anabhiratā||
soka-parideva-dukkha-domanass'upāyāsa-saŋvaṭṭa-nikesu sankhāresu anabhiratā||
jāti-saŋvaṭṭa-nike pi sankhāre nābhisankhāronti.|| ||

Jarā-saŋvaṭṭa-nike pi sankhāre nābhisankhāronti.|| ||

Maraṇa-saŋvaṭṭa-nike pi sankhāre nābhisankhāronti.|| ||

Soka-parideva-dukkha-domanass'upāyāsa-saŋvaṭṭa-nike pi sankhāre nābhisankhāronti.|| ||

Te jāti-saŋvaṭṭa-nike pi sankhāre anabhisankhāritvā||
jarā-saŋvaṭṭa-nike pi sankhāre anabhisankhāritvā||
maraṇa-saŋvaṭṭa-nike pi sankhāre anabhisankhāritvā||
soka-parideva-dukkha-domanass'upāyāsa saŋvaṭṭa-nike pi sankhāre anabhisankhāritvā||
||
jāt'andhakāram pi na papatanti||
jar'andhakāram pi na papatanti||
māraṇ'andhakāram pi na papatanti||
soka-parideva-dukkha-domanass'upāyās'andhakāram pi na papatanti.|| ||

'Te parimuccanti jātiyā jarāya maraṇena sokehi paridevehi dukkhehi domanassehi upāyāsehi,||
parimuccanti dukkhasmā' ti vadāmi.|| ||

Tasmā'ti ha bhikkhu,||
'idaɱ dukkhan' ti yogo karaṇīyo||
'ayaɱ dukkha-samudayo' ti yogo karaṇīyo||
'ayaɱ dukkha-nirodho' ti yogo karaṇīyo||
'ayaɱ dukkha-nirodha-gāminī paṭipadā' ti yogo karaṇīyo" ti.|| ||
