Aṅguttara-Nikāya
II. Duka Nipāta
XIII. Dāna Vagga

II. The Book of the Twos

XIII. Gifts

Suttas 140-149

 

[81]

Sutta 140

"Monks, there are these two gifts.

What two?

The carnal and the spiritual.156

These are the two.

Of these two the spiritual gift is pre-eminent."
