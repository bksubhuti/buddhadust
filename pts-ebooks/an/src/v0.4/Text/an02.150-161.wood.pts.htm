Aṅguttara-Nikāya
II. Duka Nipāta
XIV. Santhāra Vagga

II. The Book of the Twos

XIV. Greetings, etc.

Suttas 150-161

 

[81]

Sutta 150

"Monks, there are these two greetings.157

What two?

The carnal and the spiritual.

These are the two.

Of these two the spiritual greeting is pre-eminent."
