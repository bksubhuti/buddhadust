Aṅguttara Nikāya
Catukka Nipāta
15. Ābhā Vagga

The Book of the Fours

Chapter XV: Splendour

Sutta 144

Brilliance

 

[143]

[1][than]ed1 'Monks, there are these four brilliances.

What four?

The brilliance of the moon,
of the sun,
of fire
and of wisdom.

These are the four.

Of these four, monks, the brilliance of wisdom is the chief.'

 

ed1 No nidana is given for this sutta or this section of suttas.
