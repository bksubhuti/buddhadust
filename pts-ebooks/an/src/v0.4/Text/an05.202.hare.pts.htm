Aṅguttara Nikāya
Pañcaka Nipāta
XXI. Kimbila-Vagga

The Book of the Fives

Chapter XXI: Kimbila

Sutta 202

Dhamma-Savaṇa Suttaṃ

On Hearing Dhamma

 

[181]

Thus have I heard:

Once the Exalted One dwelt near Sāvatthī;
and there he addressed the monks, saying:

"Monks."

'Yes, lord,' they replied; and the Exalted One said:

"Monks, there are these five advantages from hearing Dhamma.

What five?

He hears things not heard;

purges things heard;

dispels doubt;

makes straight1 his view;

and his heart becomes calm.

Verily, monks, these are the five advantages from hearing Dhamma.'

 

1 Ujuṃ karoti.
