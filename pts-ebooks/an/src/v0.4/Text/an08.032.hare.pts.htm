The Book of the Eights

IV: On Giving

Sutta 32

Dutiya Dāna Suttaṃ

On giving (b)

 

[236] [161]

[1][olds][bodh]

Faith, modesty1 and faultless giving -
These are the things wise men pursue.
This is the way2 divine, they say,
The high-way to the deva-world.3

 

1 Hiriyaṃ, a rare form of hiri.

2 Magga.

3 Quoted at Kvu 341; trsl. 199; Expositor 217; Vv.A. 194.
