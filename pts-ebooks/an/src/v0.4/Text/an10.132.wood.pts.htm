Aṅguttara Nikāya

XIII: Parisuddha-Vagga

X. The Book of the Tens

XIII: Perfect Purity

Sutta 132

Dasama Suttaṃ

Wrong

 

[166]

THUS have I heard:

Once the Exalted One addressed the monks, saying:

"Monks."

"Yes, lord," they replied, and the Exalted One said:

"Monks, there are these ten states of wrongness.

What ten?

Wrong view,
wrong thinking,
wrong speech,
wrong action,
wrong living,
wrong effort,
wrong mindfulness,
wrong concentration,
wrong knowledge,
wrong release.

These are the ten states of wrongness."
