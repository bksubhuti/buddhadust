Aṇguttara-Nikāya
II. Duka Nipāta
XIV. Santhāra Vagga

Suttas 150-161

 

[81]

Sutta 150

[150.1][olds] "Monks, there are these two greetings.157

What two?

The carnal and the spiritual.

These are the two.

Of these two the spiritual greeting is pre-eminent."
