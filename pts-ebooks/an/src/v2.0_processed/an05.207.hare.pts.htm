Aṇguttara Nikāya
Pañcaka Nipāta
XXI. Kimbila-Vagga

Sutta 207

Yāgu Suttaṃ

Gruel

 

[183]

[1] THUS have I heard:

Once the Exalted One dwelt near Sāvatthī;
and there he addressed the monks, saying:

"Monks."

'Yes, lord,' they replied;
and the Exalted One said:

Gruel. A thin porridge. Made by boiling oats, wheat or rye in water or milk.

— p.p.
