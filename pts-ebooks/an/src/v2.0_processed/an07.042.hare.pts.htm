Aṇguttara Nikāya
Sattaka Nipāta
V. Mahāyañña-Vagga

Sutta 42

Satta Samādhi-Parikkhāra Suttaṃ

The Adorning1

 

[23]

[1][olds][upal]'Monks, these seven are the adornments of concentration.

What seven?

Right view,
right resolve,
right speech,
right action,
right livelihood,
right effort,
right mindfulness.

Verily, monks, one-pointedness2 of mind,
which is adorned by these seven things,
is called Ariyan right concentration
with its approach3 just thus,
its adornment just thus.'

 

1 Parikkhāra, D. ii, 216; iii, 252; M. iii, 71.

2 Ekaggatā

3 Sa-upaniso.
