Aṇguttara Nikāya

Mahāyañña-Vagga

Sutta 50

Nanda-Mātu Suttaṃ

Nanda's Mother1

 

[35]

[1][upal][than] THUS have I heard:

Once the venerable Sāriputta and the venerable MahāMoggallāna
with a company of the monks of the Order
walked a walk near2 South Hill;
and it was then that Nanda's mother,
a lay-disciple of Veḷukaṇḍa,3
rose before dawn one night and sang the 'Way to the Beyond.'4


Vessavana Kuvera, King Of The Yakshas,
And Regent Of The North
