Aṇguttara Nikāya
VIII. Aṭṭhaka Nipāta
V. Uposatha Vagga

Sutta 43

Visākh'uposatha Suttaṃ

Visākhā

 

[255] [174]

[1][khan][bodh] THUS have I heard:

Once the Exalted One was staying near Sāvatthī, at the terraced house of Migāra's mother.

This opening may cause confusion. Visākā was called 'Migāra's Mother' because she was responsible for converting Migāra, her father-in-law; but she also had a son named Migāra.

— p.p.
