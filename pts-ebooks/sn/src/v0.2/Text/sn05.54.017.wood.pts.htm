Saṁyutta Nikāya:
V. Mahā-Vagga
54. ĀnāpānaSaṁyutta

Kindred Sayings About
In-Breathing and Out-Breathing

Sutta 17

Saṁyojanam Suttaṁ

Fetter

 

 

THUS have I heard:

Once the Exalted One was staying near Sāvatthī at Jeta Grove, in Anāthapiṇḍika's Park.

Monks, intent concentration on in-breathing and out-breathing conduces to the abandoning of the fetters.
