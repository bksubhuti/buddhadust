Saṁyutta Nikāya:
V. Mahā-Vagga
54. Ānāpāna Saṁyutta

Kindred Sayings About
In-Breathing and Out-Breathing

Sutta 18

Anusayam Sutta

Tendency49

 

 

THUS have I heard:

Once the Exalted One was staying near Sāvatthī at Jeta Grove, in Anāthapiṇḍika's Park.

... It conduces to the utter destruction of tendency.

 

 

49Anusayo. Cf. text, p. 60. There are seven of these 'inclinations.'
