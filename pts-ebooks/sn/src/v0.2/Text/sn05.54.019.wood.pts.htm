54. ĀnāpānaSaṁyutta

Kindred Sayings About
In-Breathing and Out-Breathing

Sutta 19

Addhānam Sutta

The way50

 

 

THUS have I heard:

Once the Exalted One was staying near Sāvatthī at Jeta Grove, in Anāthapiṇḍika's Park.

... It conduces to thorough knowledge of the way.

(The three above to be developed as in next sutta.)

 

50Addhāna. Cf. text, 28.
